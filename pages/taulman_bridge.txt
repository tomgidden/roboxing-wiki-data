===== Taulman Bridge =====

  * [[WP>Nylon]]

==== Properties ====
  * Easy to print;  easier than [[ABS]]
  * Shrinkage: slightly higher than [[ABS]]: 0.6%
  * Does not dissolve in acetone
  * Temperature range: 217ºC (melting); 242ºC (printing)
  * Glass transition: 52ºC
  * Bed temperature: 30ºC - 65ºC
  * Ambient temperature: 30ºC - 100ºC
  * Hygroscopic: keep sealed with [[dessicants]], eg. silica gel
  * Dyes well
  * (...comments?)

==== Availability ====
  * Available from Taulman and local suppliers: [[http://taulman3d.com/buy-bridge.html]]

==== Hints and Tips ====
  * Keep it dry and sealed when not in use.
